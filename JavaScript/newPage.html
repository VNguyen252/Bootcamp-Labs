Bologna Cake

 Mm mm DELISH!

Ingredients

  • 8 sliced of Bologna (Obviously)
  • 5 cups of Mac N Cheese (Only the finest)
  • 2 cups of Tangy Mayo
  • 4 cups of Heavy Cream
  • 2 cups of Mustard (MMmm)
  1. First you want to mix your Mac with your Mayo & Heavy Cream combo.
  2. (This is the best part!)Next you want to gently layer your bologna one after the other with a generous layer of mustard inbetween
  3. Next, we're going to wrap it all up nice and tight with a thick layer of our Creamy Mayo Mac n Cheese!

Once all is said and done, your beautiful treat should look something like this!
